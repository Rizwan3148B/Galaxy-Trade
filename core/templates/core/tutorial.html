1. HOW PROFIT & LOSS WORKS

Trading is the art of predicting price movement. Your goal is to increase your Net Worth by identifying stocks that will rise in value.

PROFIT (The Rise)

When you sell a stock for more than you paid for it.
Formula: (Selling Price - Buying Price) × Quantity

LOSS (The Fall)

When you sell a stock for less than you paid for it.
Formula: (Buying Price - Selling Price) × Quantity

2. DECODING THE CHART

The chart is your primary radar. It tracks the Price (Y-Axis) against Time (X-Axis).

Candlesticks & Accuracy

  • Green Candles: Price went UP. The bottom of the block is where it started; the top is where it ended.
  • Red Candles: Price went DOWN. The top is where it started; the bottom is where it ended.
  • The Wicks: The thin lines above/below show the highest and lowest prices reached during that time period.
  • Reading Values: Hover your cursor over any candle. The precise Open, High, Low, and Close values will appear at the top-left of the chart.

4. FEATURE TERMINOLOGY

SYMBOL

The unique code for a company (e.g., AAPL for Apple).

BUYING POWER

The cash you have available to buy new stocks.

NET WORTH

Total Value = Cash + Current Value of all owned stocks.
